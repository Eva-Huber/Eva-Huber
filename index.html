As a text book “left brain” kid, I never imagined I could find my place in the creative world. Photography led to editing, which led to digital art, which led to selling stickers through Red bubble. I have been lucky to follow my creative energy through the many talents I have found, from drawing to framing and fun DIY projects like dried flower note cards!
